* cursor
** style
Cursors have many styles.  I like =bar=, but I like =box= when i leave the window.
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
  (setq-default cursor-type 'bar)
  (setq-default cursor-in-non-selected-windows 'box)
#+END_SRC

#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
